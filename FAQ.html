Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is MVA Expert?

MVA Expert is a lead generation platform that connects accident victims and injured individuals with attorneys who may be able to help. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

2. Is using MVA Expert free?

Yes, submitting your case for review is 100% free. There’s no obligation and no upfront fee.

3. Will I be contacted by a lawyer?

Yes, if your submission qualifies, an attorney or their representative may contact you for a free case evaluation.

4. What types of cases do you handle?

We help connect individuals with attorneys for car accidents, personal injuries, worker compensation claims, and other related legal matters.

5. Is my information secure?

Yes. We take privacy seriously and use secure technology to protect your data.

6. What happens after I submit my form?

Once you submit, we review your details and match you with an attorney who may contact you for further discussion.

7. What if I already have a lawyer?

If you already have legal representation, we recommend discussing your case with your existing attorney.

8. Can I use MVA Expert outside of my state?

Yes. We support clients nationwide across all 50 U.S. states.

9. How soon will I be contacted?

Typically within 24–48 hours if your case is eligible and a legal partner is available.

10. Do I have to accept the lawyer assigned to me?

No. There’s no obligation. You can choose whether or not to proceed after the initial consultation.

11. Do you guarantee compensation?

No. Legal outcomes depend on individual case details. We cannot guarantee results.

12. Is MVA Expert a law firm?

No. We are a lead generation company that works with attorneys. We do not offer legal services.

13. How do attorneys benefit from this service?

Attorneys receive relevant, pre-qualified leads from potential clients, helping them offer legal support efficiently.

14. What if no one contacts me?

If you’re not contacted within a few days, your case may not qualify. You may still consult a lawyer independently.

15. How can I contact MVA Expert?

Please refer to the contact details at the bottom of this website or use the form on the homepage.
